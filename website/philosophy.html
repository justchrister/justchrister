Quantity creates quality.

You dont stumble upon the best solutin on a whiteboard, you do so while being in the depths. Increasing output, will increase outcomes.

Complexity is ok, complicated is not.

Well designed complex systems are only many small simple units of logic, if you can't explain it to a five year old, you've made it too complicated.

Planning is everything, the plan means nothing

The plan is essential to give the direction on where you want to go, but you need to give yourself and others the autonomy to always change direction without approval, if the road is blown to pieces.
