1 Why this exists

Balancing roles as a product lead, startup founder, friend, husband and family member demands laser focus, flawless delivery, and quality time. To keep up, I’ve built a lightweight system that helps me stay efficient and truly present.

I’m not into rigid methodologies—they often spiral into bureaucracy unless every part of your business fits their mold. As Conway said, a system reflects its organization. That inspired my “anti-methodology”: a practical, all-in-one framework blending real-world tactics, from streamlined meetings to stress reduction.
