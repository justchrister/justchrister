the method / rhythm

2.1. Roadmap

The feature roadmap should include the three top-level phases and be kept updated on a weekly basis.

2.2. Eliminate bloat

A key to fast deliveries is to scope the project down to its minimal parts, phasing the deliveries to get something out quickly while postponing as many decisions as possible.

Distractions are everywhere, and its so easy being tempted by what Karri Saarinen calls side quests (the cool things you might want to do, that are unrelated to the core purpose) needs to be completely eliminated. Dont even have them in a roadmap or a log.

paper cuts should be added to a log and delivered on in bulk.

2.3. Scope

In the design and planning phase of a feature delivery, start with a design document, and split it into smaller independent deliveries.
